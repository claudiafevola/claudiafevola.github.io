About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the MATHEXP team at INRIA Saclay, founded by the MathInGreaterParis Fellowship Programme.


Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in Mathematics at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in Leipzig, where I also did my PhD under the supervision of Daniele Agostini and Bernd Sturmfels.


I am interested in algebraic geometry and connections to combinatorics, real algebraic geometry, and nonlinear algebra. My current research focuses on algebro-geometric questions in particle physics and integrable systems.
